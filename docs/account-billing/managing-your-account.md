# Managing Your Account

Keep your profile, organization, and notification settings up to date so
LoupeFactory works the way you need it to.

## Updating Your Profile Information

Your profile holds your personal details: name, email address, and display
preferences. Keeping this information current ensures your teammates can
identify you and that system notifications reach the right inbox.

To update your profile:

1. Click your avatar or initials in the top-right corner of any page.
2. Select **Profile Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. Edit your **Full Name**, **Email Address**, or **Profile Photo** as needed.
4. Click **Save Changes** to apply your updates.

!!! warning "Email address changes"
    If you update your email address, you will receive a verification link at
    the new address. Your login will continue to use the old address until
    verification is complete.

## Managing Organization Settings

Organization settings control how your LoupeFactory workspace is configured,
including your business name, timezone, currency, and team membership.

### General Organization Details

1. Navigate to **Settings → Organization** from the left sidebar.
2. Update your **Organization Name**, **Industry**, **Timezone**, and **Default
   Currency**.
3. Click **Save** to apply changes.

### Managing Team Members

You can invite new team members or remove existing ones from the **Members** tab
within Organization Settings.

To invite a team member:

1. Go to **Settings → Organization → Members**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the team member's email address and select their role.
4. Click **Send Invite**. They will receive an email with a link to join your
   organization.

To remove a team member:

1. Locate the member in the list.
2. Click the **⋯** menu next to their name.
3. Select **Remove from Organization** and confirm.

## Configuring Notification Preferences

LoupeFactory can notify you about key events, such as low inventory alerts,
order status changes, and production updates. You control which notifications
you receive and how they are delivered.

To configure your notification preferences:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**.
2. Toggle individual notification types on or off.
3. Choose your preferred delivery method for each category: **In-App**,
   **Email**, or both.
4. Click **Save Preferences**.

### Available Notification Categories

| Category | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Inventory Alerts | Low stock warnings and reorder reminders |
| Order Updates | Status changes for customer orders |
| Production Events | Stage completions and exception alerts |
| Shipment Tracking | Delivery status updates and carrier notifications |
| Billing & Credits | AI credit usage thresholds and invoice events |
