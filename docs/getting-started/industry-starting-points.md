# Industry Starting Points

Loupe Factory includes industry-specific starter defaults so your team does not
have to build every catalog and workflow from scratch. Your chosen industry in
[Organization Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#organization-settings)
shapes the starting vocabulary, catalog structure, and production flow you will
see on day one.

## Gemstones

Loupe Factory starts gemstone businesses with a catalog built around loose
stones, lapidary work, and the support supplies used to cut, polish, grade, and
ship them. Default product names include stones such as African Amethyst,
Aquamarine, Diamond, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire, and workshop supply families for
stone prep, polishing, testing, and packing.

- Default inventory flow:
  **Materials & Supplies → Rough → Preform → Cut → Caliberated → Polished →
  Girdle Polished → Finished**
- Finished inventory types:
  **Faceted, Cabochons, Beads**
- Materials & Supplies categories:
  **Stone Mounting & Prep, Cutting & Polishing, Cleaning & Maintenance,
  Gemstone Treatments, Measuring & Testing, Safety & Protection, Packaging &
  Shipping**
- Production stages:
  **Rough Assorting, Rough Cutting, Preform, Dopping, Coarse Cut, Calibrate to
  Size, Faceting & Girdle (or Dome Shaping), Pre-Polish, Final Polish, QA &
  Testing, Finish & Pack**
- Typical starter teams:
  **Rough Assorting, Preforming, Dopping, Cutting, Calibration, Polish, QA, and
  Finishing & Packing**, with planning and production administration support

Customize this setup from [Products
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#products-settings), [Inventory
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#inventory-settings), and [Production
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#production-settings).

## Jewelry

Loupe Factory starts jewelry businesses with defaults for mounts, findings,
components, finished jewelry, and the workshop supplies needed to move from
design approval through casting, setting, finishing, and dispatch. Default
product names include ring mounts, pendant mounts, bezel cups, claws and heads,
clasps, jump rings, earring posts, chains, charms, bracelets, necklaces, and
related workshop categories.

- Default inventory flow:
  **Materials & Supplies → Design Approved → CAD/STL Ready → 3D Printed/Wax
  Model → Silicone/Rubber Molds → Wax Injected → Stone-Set Wax Model → Wax
  Trees → Cast Trees (As-Cast) → Raw Casted/De-sprued → Pre-Finished
  (File/Emery) → Fabricated Parts (no casting) → Assembled (Soldered) →
  Pre-Polished/Mass Finished → Ready for Setting → Stone Set → Final Polished →
  Plated/Coated → Hallmarked/Stamped → QC Passed → Finished → Need
  Rework/Repair**
- Finished inventory types:
  **Rings, Earrings, Necklaces, Pendants, Bracelets, Bangles & Cuffs, Anklets,
  Brooches & Lapel Pins, Charms & Beads, Cufflinks & Tie Bars, Hair Jewelry,
  Body Jewelry, Jewelry Sets, Watches & Watch Bands**
- Materials & Supplies categories:
  **Precious Metals, Gemstones, Casting & Mold Making, Cleaning & Surface
  Chemicals, Plating & Coating, Stone Setting & Assembly, Workshop Machinery &
  Equipment, Workshop Supplies & Safety, Packaging & Shipping**
- Production stages:
  **Design Sign-off & BOM, CAD & Tooling Prep, Model, Mold Making, Wax
  Injection, Cast-in-Place Stone Setting, Sprue & Tree, Invest & Burnout, Metal
  Casting, Devest & Cut, Pre-finish, Fabrication, Assembly & Solder,
  Pre-Polish/Mass Finish, Stone Setting, Final Polish, Plating/Coating,
  Hallmarking/Stamping, QA & Function Test, Clean, Label & Pack**
- Size and shape defaults:
  preset size groups for center stones, melee, bezels, gallery wire, shanks,
  posts, chains, bangles, cuffs, clasps, and more, plus shape libraries for
  heads, halos, bezels, ring mounts, pendant mounts, and earring mounts
- Typical starter teams:
  **Design & BOM, CAD/CAM, Mold Making, Wax Room, Casting, Assembly & Solder,
  Stone Setting, Final Polish, QA, and packing**, with planning, sourcing,
  branding, and administration support

Customize this setup from [Products
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#products-settings), [Inventory
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#inventory-settings), and [Production
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#production-settings).

## Auto Parts

Loupe Factory starts auto-parts businesses with defaults built for industrial
manufacturing, supplier coordination, traceability, and export-ready dispatch.
Default product names cover braking, ABS, wheel-end, axle, suspension,
air-suspension, driveline, engine, filtration, electrical, body, hardware, and
repair-kit categories.

- Default inventory flow:
  **Materials & Supplies → Raw Material → Cast/Forged Blank →
  Stamped/Pressed Blank → Machined Component → Heat Treated Component →
  Surface Finished Component → Bought-out Component → Welded/Fabricated
  Component → Sub-Assembly → Tested & Approved → Finished → Need Rework / Sort**
- Finished inventory types:
  **Braking Parts, ABS & Brake Electronics, Steering & Suspension Parts, Air
  Suspension Parts, Trailer Suspension Hardware, Suspension Bushes &
  Mountings, U-Bolts & Clamping Hardware, Wheel End & Hub Parts, Wheel & Rim
  Parts, Axle & Running Gear Parts, Driveline & Transmission Parts, Engine
  Components, Cooling System Parts, Filtration Parts, Electrical Parts,
  Rubber-to-Metal Parts, Fasteners & Hardware, Body & Lighting Parts, Service &
  Repair Kits**
- Materials & Supplies categories:
  **Steel & Alloys, Rubber & Elastomers, Plastics & Polymers, Bearings &
  Rolling Elements, Electrical & Electronics, Machining & Tooling, Surface
  Treatment Chemicals, Lubricants & Process Fluids, Packaging & Shipping, Shop
  Consumables & Safety**
- Production stages:
  **Incoming Material Inspection, Blank / Primary Forming, CNC Machining, Heat
  Treatment, Welding & Fabrication, Surface Treatment & Coating, Sub-Assembly,
  Testing & Validation, Final QA Release, Pack, Label & Dispatch**
- Size and shape defaults:
  preset industrial dimensions such as OD, ID, length, width/height, thread
  size, bolt circle/PCD, tube/hose ID, spline count, kit quantity, and standard
  forms such as round/disc, cylindrical, ring, flange, threaded, splined, and
  kit/set
- Typical starter teams:
  **Incoming inspection, casting/forging, press/stamping, CNC machining, heat
  treatment, welding/fabrication, assembly, testing, QA, warehouse/logistics,
  and procurement**

Customize this setup from [Products
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#products-settings), [Inventory
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#inventory-settings), [Orders
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#orders-settings), and [Production
Settings](admin-settings-quick-map.md#production-settings).
