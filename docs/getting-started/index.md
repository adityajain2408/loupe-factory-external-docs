# Getting Started

New to Loupe Factory? This section helps you set up your workspace, understand
the business terms used across the platform, and tailor the system to the way
your company actually works.

## In This Section

- [Account Setup](account-setup.md): Sign up, log in, and configure your
  organization settings.
- [Definitions](definitions.md): Understand the core business terms used across
  products, inventory, production, and orders.
- [Industry Starting Points](industry-starting-points.md): Review the starter
  defaults for gemstone, jewelry, and auto-parts businesses.
- [Admin Settings Quick Map](admin-settings-quick-map.md): See where to
  customize your operational language, numbering, and workflow rules.
- [Quick Start Guide](quick-start-guide.md): A step-by-step walkthrough
  covering your first inventory entry, production process, and order.

## Recommended Path

For the cleanest rollout, work through this section in the following order:

1. Complete [Account Setup](account-setup.md) so your workspace reflects your
   company identity.
2. Review [Industry Starting Points](industry-starting-points.md) to understand
   the defaults Loupe Factory can preload for your business model.
3. Read [Definitions](definitions.md) so teams across sales, operations,
   production, procurement, and finance use the same language.
4. Use [Admin Settings Quick Map](admin-settings-quick-map.md) to adapt naming,
   numbering, and workflow defaults before large-scale day-to-day use.
5. Move into the [Quick Start Guide](quick-start-guide.md) once your setup
   foundation is aligned.
