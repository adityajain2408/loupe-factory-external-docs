# Admin Settings Quick Map

If your business terminology, numbering, or workflow differs from the defaults
in Loupe Factory, start in **Admin Settings**. These are the most relevant
areas for day-one customization.

## Organization Settings

Use **Organization Settings** when you want your workspace to match the way your
company presents itself and operates in a region.

- Set your organization name, logo, industry, and domain
- Choose country, timezone, currency, and display formats
- Manage locations plus rack/bin structure
- Add departments and designations that fit your real team structure
- Maintain legal or tax details that need to appear on commercial documents

For the broader setup flow, see [Account Setup](account-setup.md) and [Managing
Your Account](../account-billing/managing-your-account.md#managing-organization-settings).

## Products Settings

Use **Products Settings** when you want to shape the catalog language your team
works from every day.

- Add, remove, or refine product types
- Manage size groups and size options
- Manage shapes and shape groups
- Set the default weight unit used in product records
- Reset product catalogs back to industry-aligned defaults when you want a
  clean baseline

This is the right place to adapt terminology for gemstone ranges, jewelry
collections, or auto-parts families without changing the daily workflow pages.

## Inventory Settings

Use **Inventory Settings** when you want to control how stock is identified and
tracked.

- Manage inventory types
- Set the inventory tag prefix
- Turn rack/bin tracking on or off
- Review stock-control policies such as low-stock alerts
- Decide whether photos, notes, or other record requirements should be enforced

For daily use after setup, continue with [Inventory
Management](../platform-guide/inventory-management.md).

## Orders Settings

Use **Orders Settings** when you want commercial documents and fulfillment
defaults to reflect how your business sells and buys.

- Set numbering prefixes for sales orders, purchase orders, and invoices
- Choose default payment terms
- Manage invoice notes and HSN-related options where relevant
- Enable the shipment carriers your team should use for fulfillment and tracking

For order execution after setup, continue with [Order
Management](../platform-guide/order-management.md) and [Shipment
Tracking](../platform-guide/shipment-tracking.md).

## Production Settings

Use **Production Settings** when you want Loupe Factory to mirror your real
production route.

- Set the default due-date offset for production jobs
- Choose the default starting stage
- Add, remove, rename, or reorder stages
- Map stages to the right inventory behavior and departments
- Review QA and tolerance policies
- Reset stage defaults when you want to return to an industry-aligned baseline

For daily execution after setup, continue with [Production
Tracking](../platform-guide/production-tracking.md).

## Recommended Use

When launching a new workspace, a practical sequence is:

1. Set the business identity in **Organization Settings**.
2. Align product language and dimensions in **Products Settings**.
3. Confirm stock tracking rules in **Inventory Settings**.
4. Configure numbering and fulfillment defaults in **Orders Settings**.
5. Match the production route to your real operation in **Production Settings**.
