    What you can do in Loupe Factory¶

    📦
    Inventory Management
    Record items, categories, and units of measure so everyone works from the same source of truth.
    🏭
    Production Tracking
    Model multi-stage processes, track inputs and outputs, and capture waste and yield where it matters.
    🛒
    Order Management
    Create and fulfill customer orders from draft through production, shipping, and completion.
    🚚
    Shipment Tracking
    Buy labels, track packages, and keep delivery status aligned with your orders, without leaving the app.
    📊
    Reporting & Analytics
    Review operational reports, charts, and exports to support decisions and audits.
    🧾
    Invoicing
    Turn fulfilled work into invoices and keep revenue aligned with what shipped.

    New here? Follow these three steps¶

    A simple path into the product

    • 1 Set up your account Create your workspace, invite teammates, and tune organization settings such as currency and timezone.
    • 2 Tour the dashboard Learn how navigation, modules, and key metrics fit together so daily work feels familiar.
    • 3 Complete the quick start Walk through a realistic flow (inventory, production, and an order) so you have context for the rest of the docs.
